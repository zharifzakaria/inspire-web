INSPIRE

Institute for Pioneering of Education and Economic Excellence

INSPIRE is a newly-established and first national Think Tank, Non Political and Non-Profit Organisation that focuses on promoting entrepreneurship and business-based education excellence.

Who We Are

First mooted in 2011 by group of young entrepreneurs, corporate leaders and academician, INSPIRE was officially established on June 12, 2012. INSPIRE acts as a brain trust that promotes a culture of entrepreneurship amongst the young, which shifts their paradigm from seeking opportunities availed to them in the already competitive job market to creating new business opportunities not just for themselves, but also fostering new engines of economic growth.

INSPIRE aims to foster this spirit of entrepreneurship by producing research papers, organising talks, conferences and workshops, as well as a host of special projects.

As a start, INSPIRE will roll out four key signature programmes and modules designed to promote the culture of entrepreneurship, enhance hard and soft business skills and strengthen business networking amongst young business people and potential business startup candidates.
